SSS
Signature Sandwich
by Siddharth

Not a recipe. A repeatable process. Two eggs, brown bread, ghee, and a strict assembly order that hasn't changed in years.

01 — The Arsenal

What goes in.

  • 2× Eggs
  • 2× Brown bread slices
  • — Ghee
  • — Tandoori mayo
  • 1× Amul cheese slice
  • 3× Jalapenos
  • — Mixed herbs
  • — Jamaican spice
02 — The Architecture

How it's built.

Step 01

The Bind

Ghee in a hot pan. Crack both eggs directly onto the bread slices already laid flat. The whites bond the bread to the protein — no flipping, no separation. One unit from the start.

Step 02

The Inversion

Flip both slices egg-side down once the whites set. The bread face toasts in residual ghee while the egg finishes from conducted heat below. Two-sided cook, single flip.

Step 03

Asymmetrical Flavoring

Tandoori mayo on one slice. Cheese centered on the other. Jalapenos scattered — not evenly, on purpose. You want uneven heat distribution per bite.

Step 04

The Dusting

Mixed herbs first — they stick to the wet surfaces. Jamaican spice on top. The order matters: herbs for aroma, the Jamaican hit for residual heat that builds after the first bite.

Step 05

The Fold

Stack. Press gently — not enough to squeeze out mayo, enough to fuse the layers. Cut diagonal. The cross-section should show distinct strata: bread, egg, cheese, bread.
